Full Case Report (Bali Drone Theft, August 2025)

Timeline of Events

  • 6 July: Checked in at Tropical Canggu Hostel with my DJI Mini 4 Pro drone.
  • 28 July: Used the drone once for filming mountains in Kintamani.
  • 1 August: Helmet theft from scooter at hostel parking (captured on camera). Reported to manager, no results.
  • 3 August: Hostel guest (Seif ben Mahmoud, Instagram: @seif_ben_mshmoudd) approached me saying he was leaving for Amed for 3 days. Later suspicious behavior noted.
  • 4 August: Drone disappeared from my bed in the hostel. I noticed missing only after a few days. This is the theft date I reported.

Additional Context

- The drone was always kept on my bed, never carried outside except once.
- Hostel staff and some guests blocked me on WhatsApp after I raised the theft issue.
- Later my clothes and personal items were also stolen, leaving me with only a T-shirt and shorts.
- I submitted reports and screenshots to Bali Police (Canggu / Kuta Utara jurisdiction).
